APPALACHIA

The Appalachians are one of the most naturally photogenic spaces I've travelled to. Whether it's the imposing beauty of a pristene waterfall, the orange gradient sunsets over the rolling hills or the myriad of colours on view in fall, the Mountains have it all for a photographer. These were taken between Black Mountain, Montreat, Asheville, and the Lake Toxaway areas.

These were all shot with a Sony Alpha 6000 mirrorless camera. This was my first time shooting digitally in years, and my first time shooting mirrorless.
