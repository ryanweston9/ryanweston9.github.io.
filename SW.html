SOUTHWESTERN
AUSTRALIA

A selection of photos from the last year and a half, spent on Australia's Southwest Coast. Featuring heavily are Margaret river and the Cape Leeuwin to Cape Naturaliste Coastline, as well as Perth metro beaches and Tordirrup Natural Park in Albany.

All taken on 35mm film using my parents old Minolta Maxxum 4 SLR circa 2002.
