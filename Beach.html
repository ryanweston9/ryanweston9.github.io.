AUSTRALIA

These are the colours that make me think of home. Red dirt, blue skies, scorched earth and crystal-blue water. Australia is an raw, untouched country once you get out of the metro areas. You can travel for kilometer after kilometer without seeing a single soul. It's an incredible place to be stranded with a camera.

All taken on 35mm film using my parents old Minolta Maxxum 4 SLR circa 2002.
