LENSVIEWING

PHOTOGRAPHY

Photography for me is the creation of a memory, because, at the end of the day, we forget 90% of the things that happen in our life. Life goes on, and more slips from our mind than sticks within in. It's why we love sitting and remembering with friends. Each of us remembers different things, different snippets from past adventures, excursions, and shennangians. I've found photography to be a way of keeping memories. A way of remembering those adventures, excursions, and shennangians that I could have forgotten.

These are a collection of the memories I've created. I shoot mainly on film as it embodies the idea of a memory; an in the moment, raw and unedited view of the present, for the past. None of these are edited, just the same as memories.
